☁️ Understanding the Problem

Windows 11 security settings sometimes block backup software from installing. If you click the installer and nothing happens, your system permission settings likely require an update. This guide helps you clear these blocks and get your data into the cloud.

Your computer needs the following specifications to run this software effectively:

  • Operating System: Windows 10 or Windows 11 (64-bit).
  • Memory: 4 GB of RAM minimum.
  • Storage: 50 MB of free hard drive space for the installer.
  • Internet: Broadband connection for initial data transmission.
  • Administrative Access: You must hold an administrator account on your PC to authorize the installation.

🛠️ Solving Common Setup Errors

If you see an error message, try these steps in order:

1. Check for Pending Windows Updates

Outdated system files interfere with new software installations. Open your Settings menu, go to Windows Update, and verify that your system is up to date. Restart your computer after any updates complete.

2. Grant Administrator Permissions

Right-click the downloaded installation file. Select "Run as administrator" from the menu. This grants the installer the authority needed to access the necessary system folders.

3. Disable Temporary Antivirus Limits

Some security software misidentifies backup installers as risks. Turn off your real-time protection briefly while you install the application. Remember to turn it back on as soon as the installation finishes.

💾 Configuring Your Backup

Once the installer runs, follow the onscreen prompts. You will sign in with your account email and password. The software scans your drive for files. Choose the folders you want to protect. The software performs the initial upload in the background. Keep your computer turned on during this phase to ensure a smooth transfer.

📈 Maintaining Your Backup

Check the software status icon in your system tray at the bottom right corner of your screen. A green icon indicates that your data is current. If the icon shows a pause symbol, click it and select "Resume Backup."
